Two time axes

Event time [valid_at, invalid_at) When the fact was true in the world. Answers “where was it in 2020?”
System time [created_at, expired_at) When the system learned it. Answers “what did we believe last March?” and powers replay.

What that buys you

Corrections supersede

A correcting fact expires the old one and stamps a back-link at write time. Nothing is silently overwritten, so the timeline stays intact.

Cited, checked answers

Generation is constrained to served facts and verified claim by claim afterwards. Unsupported claims are flagged, never quietly shipped.

Self-hostable

FalkorDB or Neo4j, local or hosted embedders, your infrastructure. The core imports only the standard library.
